What is the NRW Forum ?

The NRW’s forum is an event made especially for enterprises to present the company, as well as their products in front of more than 400 students coming from different engineering faculties in Tunisia, notably from INSAT,Sup’com, ENIT, ENIM and many more.

Students will also get to discover the newest technologies implemented in the diverse enterprises specializing in different fields.

Students will also have the chance to submit their CVs and resume if interested in working in a certain company and get a closer look at the professional life in general.

Job Interview Simulations will be held for students to know what to expect in the future.Plus a chance to have their resumes reviewed by experts to help them have a well written CV.
