Our Hackathon

One of the most important axis of our event as it requires from young engineers to put their soft and hard skills to the test. Challengers will be given the opportunity to think beyond limits and showcase their creativity and efficiency in solving modern problems. This opportunity will not only broaden their knowledge through collaborating with others but also improve their public speaking skills through pitching to promote their solution.

24h competition

participants are given a fixed time to work on problems given and come up with their own solutions

Teams

One of our main purposes is to encourage people to engage in collaborative work as team work it's a fondamental skill to have in our domain.

Mentors

Teams will be guided by mentors from different companies to ensure the good proceedings of their work.

Prototypes & specifications

To further more facilitate the procedure , participants will be given all the details needed and prototypes to have a clearer idea on the subject at hand

Pitching

Each team will chose a representative to explain the solution they think is optimal and worked on throught the competition time.

Winners

Winners will be announced in the closing ceremony where they will be handed their well earned prizes.
