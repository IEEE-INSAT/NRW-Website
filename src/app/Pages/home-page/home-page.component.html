About RAS INSAT

IEEE RAS INSAT is one of the most active in Tunisia providing activities for about 200 members and playing a vital role at INSAT ecosystem by:
  • Organizing THE ONLY robotics congress in Tunisia: National Robotics Weekend
  • Representing Tunisia Yearly in EUROBOT since 2022
  • Organizing RAS ODYSSEY, a Program to Teach 100 students embedded systems, robotics, and AI in 5 Sessions for each domain.
  • Organizing BOTCAMP, THE ONLY NDT Hackathon in Tunisia
  • Educating members and enthusiasts weekly about robotics, embedded, and IOT

What is NRW?

National Robotics weekend organized annually in June by IEEE RAS INSAT in Tunis, Tunisia, is the ONLY robotics congress in the country, attracting about 350 students. This premier event serves as a vibrant platform for future engineers from Tunisia, offering them an exceptional opportunity to engage in a wide array of activities. Participants can choose to compete in various challenges and competitions, or they can take advantage of cutting-edge workshops and insightful keynotes. NRW aims to bring together the robotics community, fostering new relationships, strengthening the spirit of innovation and challenge, and promoting skills in various technological fields.
