About RAS INSAT

IEEE RAS INSAT SBC is known as one of the best performing and most active chapters of IEEE Tunisia Section . This is thanks to its hard-working members, who constantly prove their skills and their dedication to their Student Branch. Indeed, the number of awards won attests to this: IEEE RAS chapter won the 2019 Student Branch of the Year award, awarded by Region 8, as well as the exemplary branch in Tunisia at TSYP 2018 and 2017. In addition, IEEE RAS INSAT won the 1st place of the RAS challenge in TSYP 2019, all this among many other awards that demonstrate a homogeneous and competent branch.

What is NRW?

National Robotics weekend is the annual conference organized by IEEE RAS INSAT, targeting the field of robotics, and which brings together future engineers from Tunisia in order to provide them with an exceptional opportunity to build new relationships, strengthen their spirit of innovation and challenge and promote their skills in various technological fields . We won the "The runner up position in IEEE Region8 (Europe, Middle East and Africa) Education Activities's Acceleration Program Contest" in an annual competition organized by the IEEE international.
